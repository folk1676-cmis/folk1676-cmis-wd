Clownfish

Clownfish are like the goldfish of the ocean. They are colorful and playful. Their colors come in a variety ranging from orange to black. They are the perfect pet for small childrens and first time fish owner. However, they are aggressive and territorial so if you want to raise a lot of them, it is advise to have a big aquarium tank.

Shark

Sharks are arguably one of the coolest animal on the planet! They are a carnivorous creatures that likes to live alone. Depending on the species, they can come in many different sizes and colors. Their diets are pretty obvious, they eat anything that they can kill. Obviously, this beast is not recommended for unexperience pet owner because raising a shark may have lethal consequences.

Sea Urchin

Sea Urchins are spiky. They are pretty much useless and move around at the bottom of the ocean floor. Their spikes are venomous and it hurts to be stung by them. However, they are also considered a delicacy in many parts of the world, especially the Eastern Asian culture.

Hermit Crabs are a fun pet to own. You can often change their shells when they outgrow their old ones. So if you are a person who likes to customize their pets, this is the perfect pet for you! Just imagine the crab being an underwater poodle. They are fairly simple to raise but remember, crabs can also move on land so becareful where you place them.

Octopus

Octopus are considered to be one of the smartest animal in the world. But because of its short livespan, they can't utilize their smartness. They are experts in hiding from predators because they can fit in any small hole as long as their beaks can pass through the opening. Not only that, they can also change their texture and color based on their surroundings.
